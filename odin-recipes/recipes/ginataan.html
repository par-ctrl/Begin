Ginataang Kalabasa at Sitaw

Home

Description

Ginataang Kalabasa at Sitaw refers to calabaza squash and string beans cooked in coconut milk. It is creamy and it brings a delightful twist to the fresh and bright flavors of the vegetables we already know and love. This dish can be made vegan by omitting any meat and the Knorr bouillon.

Ingredients

  • 1/2 lbs. squash cubed
  • 18 pieces string beans
  • 1 piece Knorr Pork/Chicken/Shrimp Bouillon cube
  • 3 ounces pork/chicken/shrimp
  • 4 cups coconut milk
  • 1 piece onion chopped
  • 2 thumbs ginger sliced into strips
  • 5 cloves garlic crushed
  • ¼ teaspoon ground black pepper
  • 1 tablespoon cooking oil
  • Fish sauce to taste

Steps

  1. Heat oil in a pan. Add pork (or substitute with chicken or shrimp). Cook until brown and crispy.
  2. Sauté garlic, onion, and ginger.
  3. Once the onion softens, pour the coconut milk into the pan. Stir. Cover the pan and let the liquid boil. Once boiling, adjust the heat to medium and continue boiling for 5 minutes.
  4. Add Knorr Pork Cube and squash. Continue cooking for 5 minutes.
  5. Add string beans. Cook for 5 to 8 minutes.
  6. Season with ground black pepper and fish sauce as needed.
  7. Transfer to a serving plate. Serve with warm rice.
